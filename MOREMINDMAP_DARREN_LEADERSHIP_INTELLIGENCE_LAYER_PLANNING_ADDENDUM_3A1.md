## $250M / $15M-$30M Gross Revenue Lens

The $15M-$30M annual gross revenue range is user-provided strategic ambition / valuation lens, not a verified valuation fact.

Baseline interpretation:

- A $250M valuation at $30M annual revenue implies roughly an 8.3x revenue multiple.
- A $250M valuation at $15M annual revenue implies roughly a 16.7x revenue multiple.

These multiples may be plausible only under strong assumptions such as:

- high growth
- strong margins
- recurring revenue
- strategic platform value
- defensibility
- strong retention
- capital access
- acquisition premium
- partner-capital leverage
- channel/distribution leverage
- multi-vertical platform potential

The Darren Intelligence Layer must not treat this valuation lens as guaranteed or universally correct.

It should compare the $15M-$30M ambition against:

- actual dashboard evidence
- customer revenue model
- gross margin assumptions
- recurring vs non-recurring revenue
- RRG economics
- partner capital
- channel distribution leverage
- cross-vertical expansion evidence
- multi-vertical platform potential
- acquisition or strategic premium assumptions

Truth boundary:

`$15M-$30M gross revenue supporting a $250M valuation is a scenario assumption, not a product claim or guaranteed valuation rule.`

## E->P Operating Lens

Correct definition:

- `E = Entrepreneurial`
- `P = Purposeful`

Entrepreneurial means:

- winging it
- relying on hustle, instinct, charisma, personal effort, speed, and force of personality
- useful for starting motion, opening doors, and creating energy
- dangerous when it becomes unmodeled, unmeasured, or overconfident

Purposeful means:

- operating through models
- systems
- tools
- accountability
- coaching
- ongoing education
- no hubris
- repeatable execution instead of heroic improvisation

## Darren Application

Darren's Momentum Machine profile gives him Entrepreneurial strength:

- action
- energy
- motion

- partner activation

The system should help him move from Entrepreneurial motion to Purposeful scale.

The goal is not to suppress Darren's momentum. The goal is to put models, systems, tools, accountability, coaching, and ongoing education around that momentum.

The Darren Intelligence Layer should ask:
