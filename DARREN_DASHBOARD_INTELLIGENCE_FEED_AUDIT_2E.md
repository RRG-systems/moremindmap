## Executive Answer

Most of the app-stack dashboard now participates in the intelligence loop, but not all content is consumed the same way.

Directly feeding intelligence:

- Darren Strategy Chat context pack: snapshot, latest generated strategy, outcome ledger summary, since-last summary, build-map truth, and 9-path backbone are passed into the chat route.
- Generated Strategy / Five Futures / One Move: generated by the active strategy route from the Darren snapshot and persisted as the latest strategy.
- One Move Status and Outcome Ledger: written as structured evidence and read by Strategy Chat, Since Last Snapshot, Future Movement Gate, and Adaptive Strategy Draft.
- Session Memory, Future Movement Gate, and Adaptive Strategy Draft: active adaptive-loop records and model context.

Indirectly feeding intelligence:

- Financial/admin counts, company adoption, profile/assessment indexes, unavailable revenue fields, and raw tables support the snapshot and human interpretation, but the full rows are not all passed into reasoning routes.
- Current Operating State and Five Realities completeness are deterministic frontend summaries derived from snapshot/generated data. They explain the state, but are not themselves injected as separate route context.

Explains intelligence:

- Five Realities Map, Reality Completeness Meter, E->P Lens, scenario lens, Strategy Chat launch copy, panel headers, and scaffold explanations mostly help Darren understand how the system reasons.

Captures evidence:

- One Move Status Tracking, Outcome Ledger v0, Session Memory, and confirmed chat action flows capture decisions, outcomes, notes, proof targets, signal types, signal strength, and evidence weight.

Protects confidence:

- What Not To Overclaim, Model Limits, Unavailable / Not Yet Live, Future Movement Readiness, V1 Truth Boundary, build-map limits, and no-percentage/no-autonomous-replacement copy are valid confidence boundaries.

Admin evidence store:

- Behavior Profiles table, Business Assessments table, Company Adoption, summary counters, and sales visibility data are useful source/admin evidence. They should remain collapsed/admin-facing until deliberately routed into reasoning.

Not currently fed into intelligence:

- The standalone app-stack panel component and most panel summary text.
- Reality Completeness Meter as a distinct concept.
- Current Operating State card values as their own named context object.
- Sales Visibility explanatory copy and QA/test-row visibility note.
- Full raw profile/assessment table rows as displayed.
- Full Strategic Build Map card detail, roadmap/future phase copy, and some future feature descriptions.
- Five Futures Scaffold and One Move Scaffold after generated outputs exist.
- Duplicate proof/guardrail/unavailable copy where the same concept is already represented elsewhere.

## How Much Feeds

Direct feed is strongest in the active intelligence core: Strategy Chat, Generated Strategy/Five Futures/One Move, One Move Status, Outcome Ledger, Since Last Snapshot, Future Movement Gate, Session Memory, and Adaptive Strategy Draft.

Indirect feed is substantial in the deterministic evidence layer: snapshot context, sales/adoption counts, company adoption, profile/assessment indexes, 9-path coverage calculations, path comparison, proof targets, evidence gaps, and unavailable fields.

The weakest feed areas are display wrappers, roadmap details, scaffold panels, duplicate confidence copy, and full raw admin tables.

## Feed Gaps

Strategy Chat feed gap:

- It gets the core context pack, but not the frontend Current Operating State object, Reality Completeness labels, full company adoption grouping, or full raw admin rows.

Five Futures / One Move feed gap:

- Generation uses the snapshot, not current ledger/session/adaptive artifacts. That is acceptable for v1 generation, but later regeneration should consider accepted decisions and ledger evidence through a governed review flow.

Adaptive Strategy feed gap:

- Adaptive Draft consumes latest strategy, ledger, since-last comparison, summaries, and movement assessment. It does not consume the full 9-path backbone, full sales/admin table rows, or frontend reality completeness labels.

Future Movement feed gap:

- Future Movement currently uses evidence bands from latest strategy and ledger events, plus summaries count. It does not use full company adoption grouping, full financial/admin rows, or full roadmap context.

## Future Wiring Guardrails

Do not mutate future percentages automatically. Use the governed hierarchy:

1. Display as context.
2. Inject as low-weight chat context.
3. Create an evidence candidate.
4. Require human confirmation.
5. Ledger records proof.
6. Future movement candidate is generated.
7. Only later, after governance, should any future movement scoring change.

Doctrine preserved:

- Outside discovery finds signals.
- Classification labels them.
- MORE MindMap reasons.
- Darren decides.
- Ledger remembers.
- Futures move only when evidence deserves it.
