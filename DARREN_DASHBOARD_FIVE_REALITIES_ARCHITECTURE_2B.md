## Five Realities

| Reality | Current sources | Current label | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| Financial Reality | Sales/adoption counts, profile count, assessment count, missing revenue/payment/subscription evidence | Emerging | Feed intelligence |
| Behavioral Reality | Momentum Machine operating style, Darren snapshot, One Move/session behavior when present | Emerging to Strong | Explain intelligence |
| Business Model Alignment | 9-Path Backbone, Opportunity Path Comparison, Channel Growth vs other paths, $250M scenario lens as ambition | Strong | Feed intelligence |
| Constraint Reality | Proof targets, evidence gaps, One Move Status, Outcome Ledger, Future Movement Gate | Emerging | Capture evidence for future intelligence |
| Confidence Reality | Since Last Snapshot, Outcome Ledger, truth boundaries, model limits, unavailable fields | Emerging | Protect the user from overclaiming |
